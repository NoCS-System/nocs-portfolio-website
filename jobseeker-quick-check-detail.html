App Details

Overview

JobSeeker Quick Check helps you quickly understand whether you may be eligible for JobSeeker Payment, Rent Assistance, or a Health Care Card in Australia.

No sign up required. No personal information needed. Completely free. If you are unemployed, working limited hours, or temporarily unable to work, this app gives you a fast and simple way to check your potential eligibility before applying officially.

Key Features

  • Quick eligibility check in under 60 seconds
  • Answer 12 simple Yes, No, or Not sure questions
  • Instant results shown as Likely, Maybe, or Unlikely
  • No account creation or login required
  • No personal data collection
  • Free to use with no subscriptions
  • Official links to Services Australia for accurate information
  • Works offline for eligibility checks

What You Will See

After completing the quick check, you may see:

  • Your potential eligibility for JobSeeker Payment
  • Whether you may qualify for Rent Assistance
  • If you might be eligible for a Health Care Card
  • Clear and simple explanations of each payment
  • Direct links to official Services Australia pages

This app does not apply for payments on your behalf. It helps you understand what to check next.

Who This App Is For

JobSeeker Quick Check is designed for people in Australia who are:

  • Unemployed and looking for work
  • Working limited hours and needing financial support
  • Temporarily sick or injured and unable to work
  • Unsure which government payments may apply to them
  • Wanting a quick eligibility check before applying officially
  • Looking for information about Rent Assistance or Health Care Card

How It Works

  1. Tap Start Check
  2. Answer 12 simple questions about your situation
  3. Get instant eligibility feedback
  4. View details about relevant payments
  5. Use official links to apply through Services Australia

What We Consider

The eligibility check looks at general factors such as:

  • Age requirements
  • Australian residency
  • Employment situation
  • Job search status
  • Income and assets
  • Partner status
  • Dependent children
  • Rental situation for Rent Assistance

All results are estimates based on general criteria.

⚠️ Important Information

This app is not affiliated with Services Australia or the Australian Government.

This is not official advice. Eligibility depends on individual circumstances.

Final eligibility decisions are made only by Services Australia through official applications.

This app does not guarantee approval or payment.

Always check official government sources for the most accurate and up to date information.

Privacy and Security

  • Answers are not tracked
  • Eligibility check works offline
