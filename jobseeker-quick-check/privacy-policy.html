Privacy Policy

App Name: JobSeeker Quick Check

Last Updated: January 2025

Developer: NoCS System

1. Introduction

JobSeeker Quick Check ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are proud to say that we do NOT collect, store, or share your personal information.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Answers to the eligibility questionnaire
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any other personal information

3. How the App Works

The App operates entirely on your device. All questionnaire answers and eligibility calculations are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3.1 Local Processing

  • All questions and answers remain on your device
  • Eligibility calculations are performed locally
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No data is stored persistently on your device (data is cleared when you close the App)

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

The App displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). AdMob may collect certain information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • General location information (country/region level, not precise location)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

We do not have access to or control over the information collected by AdMob. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

4.2 External Links

The App may contain links to external websites, including Services Australia. When you click on these links, you will be directed to external websites that are not controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your personal information, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal data through our App. All processing occurs locally on your device.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Stop using the App at any time
  • Uninstall the App from your device
  • Adjust your device's advertising settings to limit ad tracking
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through:

  • The App Store listing for the App
  • Any contact information provided in the App

10. Compliance with Laws

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Australia)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for users in California
  • Other applicable privacy laws

11. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no data to retain. All questionnaire answers and calculations are processed in real-time and are not stored.

12. International Users

The App is designed for users in Australia. If you are using the App from outside Australia, please be aware that your information may be processed in Australia or other countries where our service providers operate.

13. Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

14. Summary

In summary:

  • ✅ We do NOT collect personal information
  • ✅ We do NOT store your answers
  • ✅ We do NOT track your usage
  • ✅ All processing happens on your device
  • ⚠️ AdMob (Google) may collect advertising-related data (see Section 4.1)
  • ⚠️ External links may have their own privacy policies

Note: This App is not affiliated with Services Australia or the Australian Government. For official information about privacy and data protection, please visit the Services Australia website or the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.
